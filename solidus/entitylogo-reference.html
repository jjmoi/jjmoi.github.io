LOGO FORMATTING GUIDELINES

Logo

- Produce the logo at @2x to be retina ready

- City logo and seal width: 88px

- The text is set in Gotham HTF

- Text min-width: 248px; Text max-width: 390px

- If text width is between 249-390px, the logo will automatically shrink down to fit within the div

- Optically center aligned the logo to the whole text block

- If the logo has short height or wierd shape, use your good judgement

- Contact design@opengov.com for access to the fonts used to produce the logo

Download Entity Logo Template (PDF)

Color

- The entity color serves as the city branding device via interface background colors and graph color tinting

- Reds look like errors - change the entity color to city's secondary color or default to blue if there's none

- Browns also look like red - change the entity color to orange brown or yellow brown

- Greens looks neon on computer screens - add more blue or yellow to the green until it doesn't look neon
