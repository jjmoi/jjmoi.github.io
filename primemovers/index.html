Prime Movers

Designing for a more inclusive tomorrow

As an initiative to strengthen design teams for Prime Movers Lab’s portfolio companies, we created the fellowship program to foster the next generation of creative entrepreneurs committed to making the world a more inclusive place. The program attracts talents from diverse backgrounds to drive innovation in complex domains such as climate, space, advanced materials, robotics, engineered food, and life sciences.

With a 70% representation of women and international backgrounds, designers from non-traditional fields came together to collaborate on inclusive solutions as part of Prime Movers Lab’s internal team. The designers assisted companies in establishing design processes, building out the design team, and influencing executive decisions. The exposure to our core companies and direct experience working with founders also made it a great trial period to see if there was a natural fit for both sides.

The ecosystem has seen great success in supporting both our portfolio companies and the designers, aiming for lasting positive impact and accelerated growth towards inclusive leadership.

Scope
Accelerator Program, Community, Design Strategy, Design Visioning, User Research, Creative Direction, Brand Identity, Product Design
