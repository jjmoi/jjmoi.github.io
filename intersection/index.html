Intersection

Designing for a more inclusive tomorrow

As part of Prime Movers Lab's startup ecosystem, I created the Intersection designer accelerator to help build better design teams for our portfolio companies. Our early stage startup partners require not only for their founding designers to possess great interdisciplinary skills, but also being comfortable to lead projects in highly complex problem spaces. Intersection provides a diverse and inclusive platform for design leaders to thrive and experience growth to be able to drive inclusive innovation in the deep tech industries.

Intersection attracts talented individuals from all corners of the globe to work on complex problems from concept to execution across an array of industries such as climate, smart cities, energy, space, advanced materials, robotics, mixed reality, engineered food, and life sciences.

With a remarkable 70% being female and nearly half hailing with international background, the potential for collaboration is vast. Many designers also come from non traditional backgrounds such as optometry and beekeeping to philosophy, military, filmmaking, legal, chemical engineering, and music and they come together to exchange learnings on similar design challenges, and provide each other feedback, to accelerate innovative and inclusive design solutions.

We continue to expand the Intersection community and platform to provide better support for our partner companies and exceptional designers throughout their journeys. Our goal is to create a lasting impact and witness the accelerated growth of designers who will become leaders, individual contributors, and entrepreneurs that are committed to making the world a more inclusive place.
