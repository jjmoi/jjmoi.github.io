Intersection

Designing for a more inclusive tomorrow

The Intersection designer accelerator is an initiative to strengthen design teams for Prime Movers Lab's portfolio companies. The accelerator aims to foster interdisciplinary skills and design leadership abilities in early-stage startups, particularly in complex problem spaces. The program attracts talented individuals from diverse backgrounds, with a focus on deep tech in sectors such as climate, space, advanced materials, robotics, engineered food, and life sciences.

With a 70% representation of women and international backgrounds, designers from non-traditional fields come together to collaborate on inclusive solutions as part of Prime Movers Lab's internal team. The designers assist companies in establishing design processes to help building out the design process and team, and influence executive decisions. The exposure to our core companies and direct experience working with founders also make it a great trial period to see if there is a mutual fit.

The Intersection platform had great success in supporting both our portfolio companies and the exceptional designers, aiming for lasting positive impact and accelerated growth towards inclusive leadership.
