Intersection

Helping designers realize dreams

As a former school teacher in developing countries, there were too many things I wish were better.The curriculums are outdated, lack of personalized attention, and limited resources. This leaves students ill-equipped to handle more complex design challenges and fails to adequately prepare students for the industry, especially for folks from nontraditional backgrounds. Intersection is committed to foster a diverse and inclusive workforce of career switchers to drive innovation by working on client projects alongside professional designers.

Our apprenticeship program attracts talented individuals from all corners of the globe, with a remarkable 70% being female and nearly 30% hailing from underrepresented groups. These exceptional individuals are matched with our non-profit and startup partner companies, all of which share our passion for creating social impact.

We are committed to supporting our apprentices by providing them with a full 3-6 months scholarship, which can be earned through working on client projects or assisting with research. This allows them to gain invaluable hands-on job experience, while also receiving ongoing feedback, mentorship, and peer support to help them achieve their goals. Our ultimate goal is to guide our talents towards full-time employment with one of our partner companies to drive diversity and inclusion in the design and tech industry.

Some of the exceptionally talented designers have come from varied and diverse backgrounds. These individuals possess a wealth of unique perspectives that can be harnessed to develop innovative design solutions across an array of fields. The potential for creativity and excellence is vast, as designers with backgrounds ranging from optometry and beekeeping to philosophy, military, documentary, legal, chemical engineering, music, and sports, among others, bring innovative insights to the design processes and solutions.

We continue to expand our programs to provide better support to our partner companies and outstanding designers throughout their professional journeys. Our goal is to create a lasting impact and witness the accelerated growth of hundreds of designers who will become successful leaders, individual contributors, and founders over the next decade that are committed to making the world a more inclusive place.
