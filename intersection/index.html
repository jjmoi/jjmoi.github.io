Intersection

Helping designers realize their dreams

As a former school teacher in developing countries, there were too many things I wish were better.The curriculums are outdated, lack of personalized attention, and limited resources. This leaves students ill-equipped to handle more complex design challenges and fails to adequately prepare students for the industry, especially for folks from nontraditional backgrounds. Intersection is committed to foster a diverse and inclusive workforce of career switchers to drive innovation by working on client projects at ESG companies alongside professional designers.

The apprenticeship works with social impact driven non-profit and startup partners to train career switchers from a variety of fields. We provide apprentices admitted to the program receive full 6-12 months scholarship either through working on client projects or assisting with research. During the program, apprentices get hands-on job experience, ongoing feedback, mentorship, and peer support that help guide them to success with the goal of gaining them full-time employment at our partner companies. Selected from all over the world, about 70% of our apprentices are female and nearly 30% are from underrepresented groups.

To gain a better understanding of how people use the design system in practice, I studied how users jumpstart a project from scratch using the design system to identify the critical issues in the product development workflow. Collaboratrns do help teams hesitate less and build things in shorter amounts of time, it is the how and the why a group of patterns and components are stitched together that result in outstanding design.

For users to build suitable user interfaces on top of the design system, it’s essential to decide on the structure with a clear hierarchy and logical reading order. Besides, all government websites and applications shouldn’t look exactly the same because trust and recognition are not universal, and the design must instead reflect the tone and subject mateadline and body text or the amount of whitespace that people had to make everywhere previously with the base UI components. With the ability to preview a test site live in minutes, agencies can focus their efforts on more important areas like interactions and content.

Putting it to use, I realized we needed to do more to build trust among stakeholders, especially when they all are very opinionated designers and developers. Most partner agencies initially used the design systems for their websites off the shelf so there was some resistance moving to cloud.gov where they feared that they had to redo all the work. We adlssible products and services in record time. We also saw more agencies follow how we expanded on the U.S. Web Design System creating their own iterations to meet their organization and product needs.
