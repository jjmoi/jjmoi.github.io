FAQs

Fractional?
A fractional design leader is part-timer who offers strategic guidance and hands on when needed to your team without the commitment of a full-time hire.

First designer?
I am the first designer leading 0→1 design at more than a dozen startups including Vaxxinity, OpenGov, Tachyus, Halo Industries, to name a few. It’s never too early to hire your first designer.

Embedded?
I embed myself into your team and work directly with founders to provide clarity, optimism, and opportunity to shape innovative thinking.

Working style?
If I have one best design solution, I am not gonna make more design options just because you want to see them. You hire me because you want the best work, not close seconds.

Feedback and revisions?
Seamlessly blendeded into your existing tools, be it Slack, Teams (sigh~), Google Docs, Asana, etc. I use whatever you use, email and text included.

Sensitive or proprietary info and IP?
I maintain strict confidentiality. I don’t lock up the design work produced, it’s 100% yours.

Unlimited design requests?
Yes, just like a full-time employee. New requests go into your queue and I'll knock them out one by one. Although I will definitely push back on some unreasonable requests.

Turnaround time?
A couple days for most requests. Maybe a week for more complex ones.

Unlimited revisions?
Always.

Cancel anytime?
Anytime.

Refund?
Do you ask for a refund on a dinner you didn't like?

Hidden cost?
I don’t offer web or software development. If you’d like to work with a full design & dev service, I can recommend my trusted partners and I can work with you as a fractional design leader to help you review the design along the way.
