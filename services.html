FAQs

Fractional?
A fractional design leader is part-time design director who offers strategic guidance and leadership to your team without the commitment of a full-time hire. This provides cost savings and flexibility.

First designer?
I am the first designer more than a dozen startups including Vaxxinity, OpenGov, Tachyus, Halo Industries, to name a few.

Working style?
I work directly with founders and C-level leaders and I provide clarity, context, optimism, and opportunity to shape evolutionary thinking. If I have one best design solution, I am not gonna make more design options just because you want to see them. You hire me because you want to see the best work, not close seconds.

Feedback and revisions?
Seamlessly blendeded into your existing tools, be it Slack, MS Teams (sigh~), Google Docs, Asana, etc. I use whatever you use.

Sensitive or proprietary info and IP?
I maintain strict confidentiality and I don’t lock up the produced design work, it’s 100% yours.

Hidden cost?
I don’t offer website development. If you’d like to work with a full design & dev service, I can suggest my trusted partners.
