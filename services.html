FAQs

Fractional?
A fractional design leader is part-time design director who offers strategic guidance and leadership to your team without the commitment of a full-time hire.

First designer?
I am the first designer leading 0→1 design at more than a dozen startups including Vaxxinity, OpenGov, Tachyus, Halo Industries, to name a few. It’s never too early to hire your first designer.

Working style?
I work directly with founders and C-level leaders and I provide clarity, context, optimism, and opportunity to shape evolutionary thinking. If I have one best design solution, I am not gonna make more design options just because you want to see them. You hire me because you want the best work, not close seconds.

Feedback and revisions?
Seamlessly blendeded into your existing tools, be it Slack, Teams (sigh~), Google Docs, Asana, etc. I use whatever you use, even email and text.

Sensitive or proprietary info and IP?
I maintain strict confidentiality. I don’t lock up the design work produced, it’s 100% yours.

Unlimited design requests?
Yes, just like a full-time employee. New requests go into your queue and I'll knock them out one by one. Although I will definitely push back on some unreasonable requests

Turnaround time?
A couple days for most requests. Maybe a week for more complex ones.

Unlimited revisions?
Always.

Cancel anytime?
Anytime.

Refund?
Would you ask for a refund for the meal you ate but didn't like?

Hidden cost?
I'm pretty transparent like an open book. I don’t offer website or software development. If you’d like to work with a full design & dev service, I can recommend my trusted partners.
