Raytheon

Advancing space and defense systems

RTX, previously known as Raytheon Technologies, stands at the forefront of cutting-edge technologies and innovations, encompassing areas such as artificial intelligence and machine learning for the aerospace, intelligence, and defense industries. The DX division is responsible for providing design and product development to all core business units within.

I led the efforts in visioning and product design specifically focusing on connected enterprise, visual factory, and data platform development. These enterprise applications aim to scale and enhance operational efficiency and data visibility.

Given the nature of highly regulated aerospace and defense industry, I'm unable to display the projects I have been involved in. If you are interested in the quality and integrity of my work, please refer to my past experiences showcased in my other projects.

Scope
Strategy, Conceptual Design, Visioning, Creative Direction, Brand Identity, Naming, User Experience Design, User Research, Information Architecture, Accessibility, Data Visualization
