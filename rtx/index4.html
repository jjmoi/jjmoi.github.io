Raytheon
Advancing aerospace and defense

RTX, formerly known as Raytheon Technologies, stands at the edge of innovations for the aerospace, intelligence, and defense industries. The EDX division creates visualizations of supply chain process scenarios and turn them into digital twins of the improved systems using real-time data to continuously improve efficiency and scalability for manufacturing and maintenance operations.

I joined the newly created team EDX to lead the design visions and conceptual projects, focusing on connected enterprise and digital twins used across all business units and enterprise clients to make better and faster informed decisions.

Due to the highly strict regulations, I’m unable to share confidential information relating to the projects I’m involved at RTX, please take a look at my other projects that highlight the similar skills and quality I bring to each project.

Strategy, Design Visioning, Conceptual Design, Creative Direction, Brand Identity, Naming, User Experience Design, User Research, Information Architecture, Accessibility, Data Visualization
