FAQs

Fractional?
A fractional designer is a part-timer who offers strategic guidance and hands on when needed to your team without the commitment of a full-time hire.

First designer?
I was the first designer leading 0→1 design at more than a dozen exciting startups including Vaxxinity, OpenGov, Tachyus, Halo Industries, Spring Science, to name a few. If you truly value design, it’s never too early to hire your first designer.

Working style?
I embed myself into your team and work directly with founders to provide clarity, optimism, and opportunity to shape innovative thinking. If I have one best design solution, I am not gonna make more design options just because you want to see more. You hire me because you want the best work, not close seconds.

Feedback and revisions?
Seamlessly blendeded into your existing tools, be it Slack, Teams (sigh~), Google Docs, Asana, etc. I use whatever you use, email and text included.

Sensitive/proprietary info and IP?
I maintain strict confidentiality. I don’t lock up the design work produced, it’s 100% yours.

Unlimited design requests?
Yes, just like a full-time employee. New requests go into your queue and I'll knock them out one by one. Although I will definitely push back on some unreasonable requests.

Turnaround time?
A couple days for most requests. Maybe a week for more complex ones. I also offer rush job for a fee. Need double speed? I charge double price.

Unlimited revisions?
Always.

Cancel anytime?
Anytime.

Refund?
Do you ask for a refund on a dinner you didn't like?

Hidden cost?
I don’t offer web or software development. If you’d like to work with a full design & dev service, I can recommend my trusted partners and I can work with you as a creative & design director to guide you through the whole process.
