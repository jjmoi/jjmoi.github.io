Teaching

Helping students realize dreams

It’s been a humble experience to teach and guide some of the most talented people from around the world through design education, especially people who transitioned from very different backgrounds.

For some reason, I have had multiple optometrists as my students in addition to other interesting professions such as ballerina, beekeeper, math teacher, philosophy professor, military veteran, documentary filmmaker, paralegal, biochemical engineer, violinist, chef, textile artist, federal investigator, tennis coach, and lawyer, just to name a few. A sea captain and hardcore punk music artists who happen to be twins have got to be my favorites. They went on to work at some of the best companies like FAANGS, and some of the Big 4s.
