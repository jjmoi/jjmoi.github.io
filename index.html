I’m passionate about making people’s lives more interesting and it’s reflected in my work across a variety of creative fields ranging from branding to digital products, furnitures, ceramics, books, typefaces, packaging, environmental graphics, and everything in between.

Currently based in Hong Kong Bangkok Tokyo Melbourne Singapore Brooklyn the Bay Area Houston, working as a principal designer at Tachyus. Previously at 18F and OpenGov. I continue to volunteer for social good at Elixir Labs and Tech for Campaigns and mentor aspiring designers at Out of Office Hours and Design Lab. In my past life, I’ve also been an art teacher, a graffiti artist, a capoeira practitioner, and a turtle.

I share random stuff on Dribbble, mostly cats. I Instagram some and write a few things about my design process on Medium. I tweet once a year.
