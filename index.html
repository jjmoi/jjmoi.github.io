JJ MOI

DESIGNER, INVESTOR, EDUCATOR

0→1 DEEP

45°N 122°W

Design Partner at Prime Movers Lab working with deep tech startup founders reinventing climate, smart cities, energy, aerospace, defense, AI/ML, computer vision, robotics, advanced materials, and life sciences.

Served as an innovation specialist at 18F designing digital services for the people, and a main contributor to the US Web Design System. First designer at Spring Science, Vaxxinity, Focused Energy, Gordian Bio, Tachyus, and OpenGov among other exciting startups solving complex problems. My work has been to space, recognized by the Smithsonian and World Economic Forum, and featured in NYT, CNN, BBC, WSJ, WaPo, PopSci, and a few other acronyms.

Studied interaction design, industrial design, cognitive science, and urban design. Lecturer at Pratt Institute, faculty advisor at UW, CMU, Brandeis, and OSU.

Originally from San Francisco, grew up in Hong Kong and Thailand, speaking three and a half languages. In a past life, architect, art historian, graffiti artist, capoeirista, and turtle. Currently based in the deepest woods of the Pacific Northwest. Spirit animal, mermaid. Allergic to cats.
