Based in the deepest woods of the Pacific Northwest, I'm bringing design sense to the deepest parts of tech. I’m passionate about making people’s lives more beautiful and it’s reflected in my cross-cultural design solutions across a variety of creative fields ranging from branding and digital products to furnitures, ceramics, books, typefaces, packaging, environmental graphics, and everything in between.

I've had the privilege to work with great companies including Google, Toyota, Mini Cooper, Oceanix, Space Perspective, Upward Farms, Vaxxinity, Momentus, Unlimited Tomorrow, Noble.AI, and Heliogen, to name a few.

Currently a design partner at Prime Movers Lab. Previously principal designer at Tachyus, user experience designer and innovation specialist at 18F, and design director at OpenGov. I continue to volunteer for social good at Elixir Labs and Tech for Campaigns, and mentor entrepreneurs and aspiring designers at places such as Techstars, Interaction Design Foundation, ADPList, and Designlab. In my past life, I’ve been an art history and design teacher, a graffiti artist, a capoeira practitioner, and a turtle. My spirit animal is a mermaid.

I provide mentorship to designxrs who are being impact by what's going on in the world right now, especially women & folx from URM groups. Happy to talk about anything design related or not, career path stuff, side projects, portfolio reviews, past life, cats & dogs, economincs, politics, philosophy, anything really.

I share random stuff on Dribbble, mostly cats. I Instagram some and write a few things about my design process on Medium. I tweet once a year.
