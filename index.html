Currently principal designer at Tachyus. Previously at 18F and OpenGov. I’m passionate about designing things to make people’s lives easier and more interesting.

Coming from an industrial design background, I’d approach each problem by analyzing people’s needs and goals, exploring the product’s physical qualities and the connection between disparate elements, and designing to improve the relationship between products and people.

In my past life, I’ve been designing furnitures, ceramics, books, typefaces, packaging, corporate identities, and environmental graphics. I’ve also been an art & design teacher, a graffiti writer, a capoeira practitioner, and a turtle. I continue to volunteer for social good at Elixir Labs and Tech for Campaigns and mentor aspiring designers at Out of Office Hours and Design Lab.

I share random stuff on Dribbble, mostly cats. I Instagram some and write on Medium a few. I tweet once a year.
