JJ Moi

Designer, educator, VC, and advisor to deep tech startups reinventing climate tech, smart cities, aerospace, robotics, nanomaterials, and life science.

My work has been to space, recognized by the Smithsonian and the World Economic Forum, and published in NYT, CNN, BBC, WSJ, WaPo, PopSci, and a few other acronyms. Previously innovation specialist at 18F and founding designer at Tachyus and OpenGov. Also the first designer at Vaxxinity, Focused Energy, and Gordian Bio, among other exciting startups solving complex problems.

Studied interaction design, cognitive science, and architecture. Lectured at Pratt, served as advisor and mentor at UW, CMU, and OSU. In a past life, art historian, graffiti artist, capoeirista, and turtle. Spirit animal, mermaid. Originally from San Francisco but grew up in Hong Kong and Thailand, speaking three languages. Currently based in the deepest woods of the Pacific Northwest. Allergic to cats.
