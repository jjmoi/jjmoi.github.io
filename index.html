Designer, educator, VC, and advisor to deep tech startups reinventing climate tech, smart cities, aerospace, robotics, nanomaterials, and life science.

My work has been to space, recognized by the Smithsonian and the World Economic Forum, and appeared in a number of publications, including NYT, CNN, BBC, WSJ, WaPo, PopSci, and a few other acronyms. Previously, designed digital services for the people as an innovation specialist at 18F and built the world's leading open data platforms as the founding designer at Tachyus and OpenGov. Also the first designer at Vaxxinity, Focused Energy, Upward Farms, Gordian Bio, Halo Industries, among some other early stage startups that have reached unicorn status.

Completed postgraduate studies in Interaction Design from the Academy of Art, Cognitive Science from UC Berkeley, and earned a BFA in Industrial Design from Rangsit University. Actively giving back to the design community, currently helping students realize their dreams as a lecturer at Pratt Institute as well as serving as a guest critic and mentor at University of Washington HCDE, Carnegie Mellon University HCII, and Oregon State University School of Design Arts. In a past life, art historian, graffiti artist, capoeirista, and turtle. Spirit animal, mermaid. Grew up in Hong Kong, Bangkok, and San Francisco, speaking three languages. Based in the deepest woods of the Pacific Northwest with 2 lovely kids. Allergic to cats.
