Interdisciplinary design leader, early stage VC, educator, and advisor working with deep tech companies reinventing climate, smart cities, energy, aerospace, defense, AI/ML, robotics, quantum computing, advanced materials, and life sciences.

My work has been to space, recognized by the Smithsonian and the World Economic Forum, and appeared in NYT, CNN, BBC, WSJ, WaPo, PopSci, and a few other acronyms. Served as innovation specialist at 18F designing digital services for the people and a main contributor to the US Web Design System. Founding designer at Spring Discovery, Vaxxinity, Focused Energy, Upward Farms, Gordian Bio, Tachyus, and OpenGov among other exciting startups solving complex problems.

Studied interaction design, industrial design, cognitive science, and architecture. Lecturer at Pratt Institute, faculty advisor at UW, CMU, Brandeis, and OSU. Originally from San Francisco, grew up in Hong Kong and Thailand, speaking three and a half languages. In a past life, art historian, graffiti artist, capoeirista, and turtle. Currently based in the deepest woods of the Pacific Northwest. Spirit animal, mermaid. Allergic to cats.
