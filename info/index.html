JJ Moi
Info

Designer, educator, VC, and advisor to deep tech startups reinventing climate tech, smart cities, aerospace, AI, robotics, nanomaterials, and life science.

My work has been to space, recognized by the Smithsonian and the World Economic Forum, and appeared in NYT, CNN, BBC, WSJ, WaPo, PopSci, and a few other acronyms. Served as innovation specialist at 18F designing digital services for the federal government and contributed to the US Web Design System. Founding designer at Spring Discovery, Vaxxinity, Focused Energy, Upward Farms, Gordian Bio, Tachyus, and OpenGov among other exciting early stage startups solving some of the most challenging problems.

Studied interaction design, industrial design, cognitive science, and architecture. Lecturer at Pratt Institute, advisor and mentor at UW, CMU, Brandeis, and OSU. Also co-founded Intersection, a design apprenticeship program fostering the next generation of creative entrepreneurs committed to making the world a more inclusive place.

Originally from San Francisco, grew up in Hong Kong and Thailand, speaking three and a half languages. Currently based in the deepest woods of the Pacific Northwest. In a past life, art historian, graffiti artist, capoeirista, and turtle. Spirit animal, mermaid. Allergic to cats.
