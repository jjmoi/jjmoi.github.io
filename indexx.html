JJ Moi

Interdisciplinary design leader, early stage VC, educator, and advisor working with deep tech companies reinventing climate, smart cities, energy, aerospace, defense, computer vision, robotics, advanced materials, and life sciences.

Served as an innovation specialist at 18F designing digital services for the people, and a main contributor to the US Web Design System. First designer at Spring Science, Vaxxinity, Focused Energy, Gordian Bio, Tachyus, and OpenGov among other exciting startups solving complex problems. Studied interaction design, industrial design, cognitive science, architecture, and urban design. Lecturer at Pratt Institute, faculty advisor at UW, CMU, Brandeis, and OSU.

My work has been to outer space, recognized by the Smithsonian and the World Economic Forum, and appeared in NYT, CNN, BBC, WSJ, WaPo, PopSci, and a few other acronyms.

Originally from San Francisco, grew up in Hong Kong and Thailand, speaking three and a half languages. In a past life, architect, art historian, graffiti artist, capoeirista, and turtle. Currently based in the deepest woods of the Pacific Northwest. Spirit animal, mermaid. Allergic to cats.
