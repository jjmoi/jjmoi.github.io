Founding Designer

User feedback like thumbs up/down, edits, or regenerations is just noise if you can’t act on it. Lami turns that noise into a clear, actionable signal by transforming messy interactions into structured and traceable feedback.

Expert in The Loop

AI only works when domain experts are at the center. Expert-in-the-loop turns AI from brittle demos into transformative tools by grounding it in real workflows and stakes. To build the future, we have to design with specialists as the baseline and requirement.
