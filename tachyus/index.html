Tachyus

Optimizing oil and gas production

The oil and gas industry is one of the most critical industries on the planet. It’s the reason most of us can go places. Tachyus creates technology to optimize oil and gas production with machine learning and physics.

During the years as a founding and solo designer, I collaborated with a team of software developers and petroleum engineers to revolutionize the oil and gas industry by leveraging data to make profitable decisions. We analyzed a massive amount of data from oil reservoirs around the world to offer multiple best possible future scenarios.

With little access to end users, I initiated UX trainings and workshops to empower our sales engineers to sneak in some user research when they meet with customers. We also collaborated with petroleum engineering faculty at top universities to understand the challenges faced by different professionals through empathy workshops.

In addition to the design principles and design systems to accelerate development, I also created an automated system to generate accessible color palettes for the various data visualizations. We also provided bidirectional text support to serve our diverse customer base since localization was also a top priority.

The response from customers was overwhelmingly positive, sparking industry conversations around the value of our predictive models. Our solutions delivered remarkable results, with up to a 20% increase in production and up to a 40% decrease in costs across 30,000 oil wells, particularly in complex reservoirs.
