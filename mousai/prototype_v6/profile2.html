About the project

We are a Swedish ambient band and we have been active since 1994. Formed in San Francisco, we consist of Lynn Bishop (vocal, ukulele), Joseph Lauden (bass), Olga Van Nulrson (DJ, synth) and Boyd Thana (drums, keyboard).

We are collaborating with the finest artists in the Bay Area and we’ve produced about 70% of our album. We are looking forward to finish the album production and music videos editing.
